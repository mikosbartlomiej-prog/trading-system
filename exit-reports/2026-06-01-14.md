## Critical Bug: hold_hours=0 for crypto positions

Payload reports `hold_hours=0` for LINKUSD, SOLUSD, UNIUSD despite actual holds of 35.8-39.8h. The exit-monitor's hold_hours computation is not correctly determining entry time for crypto positions with `strategy=unknown`. This causes:
- CLOSE_DECAY recommendations never firing for these positions
- P&L drift untracked across sessions

**Recommended fix:** In `exit-monitor/monitor.py`, fall back to `position_mfe[symbol].first_seen_at` when `hold_hours < 1` and MFE data is available with `first_seen_at` significantly older.
